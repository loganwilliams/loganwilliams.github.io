Logan Williams

As Though I Were There

About the poem. If a landmark unrecognized does not exist, a non-existent landmark recognized becomes one. Cross the sidewalk and crest the ridgeline. Continue straight, past the library and through the marsh thistles. Follow the stream down the street. Knock on the door and collapse on the summit.

What topographic ritual summons city from chaparral? Where is the boundary between psychogeography and psychogeographical break?
